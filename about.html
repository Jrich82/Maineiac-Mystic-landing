§ I — How It Works

Readings, generated locally.

Maineiac Mysticbot runs inside Telegram — no app to download, no account to create beyond what you already have. You pick a reading, pay through Stripe, and within seconds a full reading arrives in your chat.

The bot is powered by a large-language model running locally on a machine in Bangor, Maine — not sent to a cloud server. Your question, your name, your birth details: they travel to Maine and nowhere else.

Every reading is generated fresh at the moment you ask. No pre-written templates, no copy-paste. The model is tuned to the Maineiac Mystic persona — thirty years by the water, reading the tides, reading people.

Readings are not stored after delivery. They live in your Telegram chat, and that's it.

§ II — The Builder

Built in Bangor.

Maineiac Mysticbot was built by a developer based in Bangor, Maine — the same granite-coast city that inspired the Mystic persona. It started as a personal project: a way to make something that felt genuinely local and genuine in its voice, rather than another generic AI chatbot.

The model, the bot, the persona, the infrastructure — all of it runs on hardware in Maine. When you send your question, it goes to Bangor, gets answered by a model that's never touched a cloud GPU, and comes back.

This isn't a VC-backed platform. It's one person, a good machine, and the belief that a well-crafted AI reading is worth something.

§ III — Questions

Frequently Asked

Is this real psychic reading, or is it AI? ▶
It's AI — specifically a large-language model running locally in Maine, channeled through the Maineiac Mystic persona. The readings are not coming from a human psychic. What the bot offers is genuine in its voice and effort: every reading is generated fresh for your exact question, in the Mystic's tradition. Whether that's "real" is for you to decide.
How much does a reading cost? ▶
Readings run $2.99–$4.99, paid in-chat when you choose one. Tarot, Numerology, and Moon Phase are $2.99; Psychic and Dream Interpretation are $3.99; the full Birth Chart is $4.99. No subscription, no minimum — pay per reading.
How long does a reading take? ▶
Most readings arrive in under 60 seconds from the moment you submit your details. Birth chart readings sometimes take slightly longer — there's more to calculate. The bot will let you know it's working while it generates.
Is my information private? ▶
Yes. The bot runs on a self-hosted machine in Bangor, Maine — your details are not sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or any other third-party AI provider. Readings are not stored after delivery. Payment is handled by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy. Your Telegram account details are handled by Telegram.
What payment methods are accepted? ▶
Card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.) via Stripe, or Telegram Stars — Telegram's built-in digital currency. Both options are presented in the bot when you select a reading.
What if I'm not satisfied with my reading? ▶
Message the bot directly at @MAINEIACMYSTICBOT and explain what fell short. Refunds are handled case by case.
Do I need a Telegram account? ▶
Yes — Telegram is the delivery channel. If you don't have one, creating an account is free and takes about two minutes at telegram.org.
